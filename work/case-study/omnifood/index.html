Omnifood Digital Rebrand

For most of its existence, Omnifood stood alone atop the home cooked food delivery category. As Omnifood's Agency of Record, Splice helped expand and reinforce that position over a one-year period that saw growth in the metropolitan areas of South Africa. But, as the competition caught wind of an opportunity, a slew of rivals soon burst on the scene to spark a price war. Here’s how we helped Omnifood maintain category leadership with its highest-ever market share, while upholding a premium price point in the midst of a price war.

Web Redesign: A Hub For the ‘Believer’ Community

Propelled by a goal to drive trial for new flavors, the redesigned omnifood.co.za provides a modern hub for product information and community news. Omnifood’s diverse family of foods is presented within a dynamic “virtual shelf” that allows a visitor to quickly find a juice by simply selecting a category, flavor or benefit.

Social Media: Building a One-on-One Dialogue

“Bare Believers” make up an active and passionate community that consistently shares its experiences with Omnifood products. Our curated social engagement editorial calendar helps Omnifood meet them where they are – on their terms. By participating in an authentic two-way conversation and supplementing it with compelling content, we maintain relevance and reinforce brand equity drivers.
